About

Research-informed. Learner-centered. Built for scale.

I'm an education program manager and learning designer with six years of experience based in Barcelona. My work sits at the intersection of SLA theory, instructional design, and emerging technology.

I design the way I was trained to teach — start with the learner, understand the gap, then build something that actually closes it. Needs analysis comes first. Then I move through the design process, ADDIE/SAM, grounding instructional decisions in Task-Based Learning, Krashen's Input Hypothesis, and Vygotsky's sociocultural theory. I write clearly, design cleanly, and collaborate at every stage rather than disappearing and reappearing with a finished product. The result is research-informed and built for real people in real contexts, applied to classroom and digital contexts.

I've designed for multilingual learners, trained teachers at scale, and built an AI coaching agent from scratch. I'm currently pivoting toward learning design roles in language learning apps and edtech platforms.
